Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the event

What is included in my ticket?
All tickets include access to keynote sessions, networking events, meals and refreshments, event materials, and swag bag. Professional and VIP tickets include additional workshops, recordings, and exclusive perks. Check the pricing section for detailed comparison.
Can I get a refund if I can't attend?
Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. After that, tickets can be transferred to another person or credited towards next year's event. Contact our support team for assistance.
Is there a dress code for the event?
Business casual attire is recommended for daytime sessions. The evening reception is business formal. Comfortable shoes are suggested as you'll be moving between sessions throughout the venue.
Will the sessions be recorded?
Professional and VIP ticket holders receive access to session recordings for 90 days after the event. Early Bird tickets do not include recording access, but it can be added for an additional fee.
Are there networking opportunities?
Absolutely! We've built in dedicated networking time throughout the event including coffee breaks, lunch sessions, evening receptions, and a mobile app to connect with other attendees before and during the event.
What COVID-19 safety measures are in place?
We follow all local health guidelines. The venue has enhanced air filtration, hand sanitizing stations throughout, and spacious layout allowing for comfortable distancing. We'll update attendees with current protocols closer to the event date.
